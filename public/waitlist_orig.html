Last update: January 18, 2008

This list will be updated at least once each month. Your position may go up or down, due to the fact that siblings and learners in the San Carlos School District receive priority over non district learners.

For example, if you are an out of district learner at position 14 and an application is received for a learner residing in the San Carlos district, you will be moved to wait list position number 15.

Another example, if your child is an in district learner on the 3rd grade list in position #5, and the learner in position #14 has a sibling who is admitted in another grade, then the learner at position #14 will move to the front of the list with other siblings, moving your learner to position #6.

The office staff does not keep track of changes to the wait list. If you call they will not be able to tell you of any changes. The wait list is maintained by the enrollment team, who will update the online list as changes are made.
